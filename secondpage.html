Star Wars: Episode V

An imperial star destroyer exits lightspeed too close to the planet of Hoth. Hoth is occupied by rebel forces from which the imperium is very fond of getting rid of and have now been alerted by the huge ship in their atmosphere. Lord Vader gets word from one of his generals about this failure and contacts the man responsible. He then presumes to force choke the admiral to death while on the video call and reinstates the officer next to the dying admiral to his now former rank.

This scene highlights the ruthless nature of Darth Vader while also letting the comedy of the Star Wars movies shine through by being so absurd.
