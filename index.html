Star Wars: Episode IV

Here I describe my favourite scene in each movie and talk about it a little bit.

A brutal onslaught of stormtroopers are taking over and killing the rebel troopers. Through the smoke there emerges a figure, tall and dark. On him there are black robes and a helmet that is recognized by everyone. It’s Darth Vader, the dark lord of the Sith.

When we, for the first time ever, see this scene it is immediately apparent to us who and what kind of character we’re dealing with. He is evil and represents the enemy. He’s the big baddie! I like this scene for its simplicity and for the old charm that the original Star Wars movies had and how effortlessly it conveys to us, the viewer, what it wants us to see.
